FAQ - Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is MEDICLAB?

MEDICLAB is a smart health-tracking application designed to help you monitor and analyze key aspects of your well-being, including sleep patterns, physical activity, and overall health status. The app provides insights based on your personal data, helping you make informed lifestyle decisions for better health.

2. How does MEDICLAB track my health?

Our app gathers data from multiple sources, including:

  • Your smartphone’s built-in sensors (such as step counters and accelerometers).
  • Wearable devices and health tracking integrations.
  • Manual inputs you provide, such as sleep duration, mood, or symptoms.
We use advanced algorithms to analyze this data and provide trends, alerts, and recommendations tailored to your health goals.

3. Can I use MEDICLAB without a wearable device?

Yes! While MEDICLAB integrates with popular wearables for more precise tracking, you can also manually enter your data or rely on your phone’s sensors for basic tracking.

4. How does MEDICLAB help me improve my health?

The app provides data-driven insights, trend analysis, and personalized suggestions based on your activity levels, sleep quality, and health trends. Additionally, it can notify you of irregular patterns or suggest changes to improve your well-being.

5. Is my data private and secure?

Absolutely. We take data security very seriously. Your personal health data is encrypted and stored securely. We do not sell or share your data with third parties without your explicit consent. You are always in control of your information.

6. Does MEDICLAB provide medical advice?

No, MEDICLAB is a health-tracking tool, not a medical device. It does not provide medical diagnoses or treatment recommendations. If you have any concerns about your health, we encourage you to consult a healthcare professional.
